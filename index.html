About Me

Hi, My name is Luke Firth. So nice of you to stop by my page! If you have made it this deep into my website and have found yourself reading my “about me” blurb then I am assuming you want to know a little more. So let's get to it!


I like to create and construct things. Having worked previously as a Graphic Designer, CNC Programmer & CAD Draftsman I was able to manufacture almost anything I conjured up with my mind (The general laws of physics and gravity kept some things off limits. No fire breathing dragon-robots, but you get what I mean). I am constantly inspired by emerging technologies and new innovations. These are my biggest obsessions and creative motivators.


After eight years at my last company I felt it was time to go on a journey of self discovery and start a fresh new chapter of life. Growing, learning and honing in on my passions became my main focus. This took some self reflection as I had to consider 1) what I am most ambitious about 2) whether or not can I make any money doing that thing and 3) will my girlfriend stay with me if I say I want to write code every single day for the rest of my life?


So I quit my full-time job and ventured into the world of unemployment (my girlfriend only cried once and yes she stayed with me) and I signed up to General Assembly's twelve week immersive Software Engineering course. I didn't have a social life for twelve weeks but it was the most alive I have ever felt, being challenged every day and developing into the super nerd that I am today. Studying and working as a Software Engineer has given me the ability to explore my creativity in a new way, being able to mix logic with design.


In my down time I enjoy diving into a good book (always taking recommendations) and drinking expensive coffee whilst eating smashed avo on toast at overpriced cafes (cue the millennial jokes). If you're interested in working with me I would love to enjoy an organic, fair trade coffee with you sometime. Please enquire within.


Always and never not sincerely,


Your new tech savvy mate, Luke Firth
